# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 27, 2026

GoFlag ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

## Data Storage

GoFlag does not operate any servers and does not collect or store personal data on external infrastructure. All user data — including usernames, goals, friend connections, comments, and reactions — is stored exclusively in users' personal iCloud accounts via Apple's CloudKit service. This data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) and remains under user control at all times.

## Data Collected

The following information is stored in your iCloud account when you use GoFlag:

- **Account information:** A username you create within the App
- **Flags:** Goals you create, including text, target dates, and completion status
- **Social data:** Friend connections, comments on flags, and emoji reactions
- **Preferences:** App settings and notification preferences

## How Data Is Used

GoFlag accesses your iCloud data solely to provide the App's core functionality:

- Displaying your flags and timeline
- Enabling friend connections and shared flags
- Delivering comments and reactions between connected users
- Sending push notifications about friend activity (if enabled)

The App does not have direct access to user data beyond what is necessary to provide these features. All data remains within Apple's CloudKit infrastructure and individual iCloud accounts.

## Third-Party Services

GoFlag does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. No user data is shared with or sold to third parties.

The App relies on the following Apple services:

- **CloudKit:** For data storage and synchronization
- **Apple Push Notification Service (APNs):** For delivering notifications
- **Sign in with Apple:** For authentication (if enabled)
- **App Store / StoreKit:** For subscription management

These services are governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Payments

GoFlag offers an optional Premium subscription, processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. No payment information is collected, processed, or stored by the App. All billing, refunds, and subscription management are handled by Apple and subject to Apple's terms.

## Data Retention and Deletion

User data persists in iCloud as long as the App is used. Data can be deleted in the following ways:

- **In-App:** Settings → Account → Delete Account permanently removes all flags, comments, reactions, and friend connections
- **Via iCloud:** Users may manage or delete App data directly through Apple's iCloud settings at any time

Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

## Children's Privacy

GoFlag is intended for users aged 13 and older. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If it becomes apparent that such data has been inadvertently collected, it will be deleted promptly.

## International Users

**GDPR (European Union):** GoFlag does not store data outside of users' personal iCloud accounts. Users may exercise rights to access, correct, or delete their data directly through the App or Apple's iCloud settings.

**CCPA (California):** GoFlag does not sell personal information. All data is stored in users' personal iCloud accounts under their direct control.

## Security

Data security is provided by Apple's CloudKit infrastructure, which includes encryption in transit and at rest. The App does not have access to authentication credentials or decryption keys.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the App following any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
